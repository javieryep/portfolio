About

ProLife Brand is a grass-roots initiative started by Kai and Kate Yep. Its goal is to promote a clear, recognizable and well-crafted pro-life symbol that serves as a unifying visual identity for the pro-life movement.

We believe that the appropriate communication of the pro-life message is fundamental to help end abortion and protect the lives of pre-born people. However, even after decades, the pro-life movement still does not have a distinct and unified visual identity. More over, we perceive that the current state of the pro-life message is often fragmented and ineffective.

To learn more, visit the project website and the Instagram account.
