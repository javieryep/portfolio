Background

Most people are illustrators, at least for the early years of their lives. I wasn’t any different, but consistency annd some talent gave me the chance to try different techniques, materials and styles. Here’s a collection of visual pieces I’ve made so far. They include digital and physical pieces, illustrations, and even logos. I hope you enjoy them.

Remote Work Daily Tips

I contributed illustrating these remote work daily tips. Each one was posted on a different day with recommendations on best practices for working remotely.

Different techniques

During a class in design school I was exposed to a multitude of materials and techniques. All these are originally non-digital pieces.

Calligraphy

I learned about Gothic calligraphy and fell in love with it, especially the Fraktur type. I taught myself how to do it and the final outcome was these calligraphy posters.

Religious

During my time in the Catholic seminary, I created some religious art pieces. These include two big marble mosaics and some paintings.

Logos

The gateway drug to design for me were logotypes and brands. I became a freelance brand designer.
